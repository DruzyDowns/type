Susan Kare and the first Apple GUI

Susan Kare is an artist and designer best known for creating UI elements and typefaces for the first Apple Macintosh from 1983 to 1986.

Kare was working as a sculptor and curator when her high-school friend Andy Hertzfeld asked her to create graphics for a new computer that he was working on in California.

“It just so happened that I had small black and white grids to work with. The process reminded me of working needlepoint, knitting patterns or mosaics. I was lucky to have had a mother who enjoyed crafts.”
—Susan Kare
