Hermann & Gudrun Zapf

Hermann Zapf's career spanned decades of progress, from traditional lead type, to the inception of the Apple Mac and beyond.

Dingbats, a collection of symbols for supplementing type, was a stepping stone in the path to the modern emoji we know and love today.

Zapf was an early adopter of computing technology, but was dismissed by his colleagues as crazy for his interest in producing type digitally.
