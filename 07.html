OpenType and Variable fonts

In the past, a typical Western PostScript font was limited to 256 glyphs, but with a single cross-platform OpenType file, you can include more than 65,000!

It also allows near infinite possible combinations of weights and styling.

Inter is a an open-source variable font with an interactive demo.
